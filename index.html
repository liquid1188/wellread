What the press is saying

📰
The Elite College Students Who Can't Read Books
The Atlantic · Rose Horowitch · October 2024
Professors across elite universities report students arriving unable to finish even short novels — a crisis of reading stamina driven by smartphone culture and declining high school standards.
 📰
Americans Are Reading Fewer Books for Less Time
NPR · February 2025
Americans read an average of three fewer books per year than five years ago. The share reading daily fell from 28% to 16%.
 📰
Americans Reading Fewer Books Than in Past
Gallup
Average books read per year dropped from 16 to 12. The decline is greatest among college graduates — the demographic that once read the most.
